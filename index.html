The Easiest Way to Buy & Sell Bitcoin Peer-to-Peer

Peach Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer mobile app for trading Bitcoin anonymously: we do not conduct any Personal Identification processes (No KYC - Know Your Customer). Peach Bitcoin simply connects buyers and sellers together in a secure and private way! And it’s FREE to sell Bitcoin on Peach!!!

Global & Local Payment Methods Available!

Buying and selling peer-to-peer means that you use the payment methods of your choice to pay and get paid. The buyer sends the fiat money directly to the seller’s specified account. Peach does not hold any user’s money at any point in time. The Bitcoin(s) are locked in an escrow to ensure a safe transaction for both parties.

Whether you're looking to buy Bitcoin with a gift card or prefer the simplicity of purchase without ID, Peach provides a trusted platform for conducting anonymous transactions and peer-to-peer exchanges.

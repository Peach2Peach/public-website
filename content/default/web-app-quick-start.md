# Web App Quick Start

@[toc]

## Intro: Your phone holds the keys, your desktop gets the screen

The Peach web app at [web.peachbitcoin.com](https://web.peachbitcoin.com) is a **companion to the mobile app**, not a replacement. Your Peach account — and the keys to your bitcoin — live on your phone. The web app pairs with it, just like WhatsApp Web: you browse the order book, create offers and manage trades comfortably on your desktop, while every sensitive action is signed and confirmed on your phone. Self-custody and no-KYC stay exactly the same.

## Guide 1: Install the mobile app and create your account

If you don't have Peach on your phone yet, install it through the [App Store]($iosUrl$), [Play Store]($androidUrl$), [APK download](/apk/) or [Zap Store](https://zapstore.dev/) and create your account. It only takes a few seconds — see the [mobile Quick Start guide](/quick-start/) for the full walkthrough. Don't forget to [create a backup](/faq/account/#how-should-i-store-my-backup)!

## Guide 2: Pair your browser

:::quickstarts 4
![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/pairing/01-open-web.webp)
On your desktop, open **[web.peachbitcoin.com](https://web.peachbitcoin.com).** A pairing QR code is shown. **Always double-check you are on the correct URL**

![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/pairing/02-scan-icon.webp)
On your phone, open the Peach app and tap "Connect to desktop" (on Android you'll find it in the settings)

![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/pairing/03-scan-qr.webp)
Point your phone's camera at the QR code on your desktop screen. TIP: you may have to get really close to the QR (especially for GrapheneOS users).

![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/pairing/04-paired.webp)
That's it, your browser is paired, just like WhatsApp Web. You'll see the homepage with different sections like news, stats and your profile summary
:::

## Guide 3: Trade from your desktop

:::quickstarts 4
![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/trading/01-order-book.webp)
Browse the full order book and filter offers by currency and payment method

![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/trading/02-create-offer.webp)
Create your own buy or sell offers with your preferences

![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/trading/03-manage-trades.webp)
Send and accept trade requests, chat with your counterparty and keep an eye on your ongoing trades

![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/trading/04-confirm-on-phone.webp)
When an action needs your keys — like releasing the escrow — you confirm it on your phone. Your keys never leave your phone
:::

## Guide 4: Disconnect a session

Ending a session is easy: simply closing the window logs you out. This is especially handy on a shared or public computer.

:::quickstarts 1
![](/img/faq/web-quickstart/sessions/log-out.webp)
You can also log out at any time with the button in the top right corner
:::
